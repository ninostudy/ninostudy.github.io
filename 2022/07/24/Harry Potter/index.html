IELTS写作素材：Harry Potter

Harry Potter

词语：

Perilous adj.危险的

Perilously adv.危险的

eg. We came perilously close to disaster.

我们险些出了大乱子。

emetic n.催吐剂 adj.呕吐的

score n.配乐

Ersatz adj.人造的

wax-sealed adj.蜡封的

come of age 达到法定年龄

eg. Come of reading or viewing age

Reunion n.重聚

Aesthetic n.美感，美学

from the off 从一开始

set piece n.固定套路

a rush of sth. 一种迸发的情感

nostalgia n.怀旧

nostalgic adj.怀旧的

Conspicuous adj.显眼的

be conspicuous by one’s absence

ideology n.意识形态

denounce vt.谴责

denounce sb. as sth.

well-worn adj.听腻了 =cliched

anecdote n.轶事、趣闻

True to form 和往常一样

don’t tell a soul 谁也别告诉

tangential adj.不直接相干的

endeavour n.努力 =attempt/effort

palpable adj.明显的、可察觉的

slick adj.徒有其表的

It provides a rush of nostalgia only heightened as the enduring affection among them all becomes evident.
