IELTS写作素材：The art of fasting

The art of fasting

Words：

fast v.节食、斋戒

abstinence n.节制、禁欲

abstain v.戒除 ～from sth.

fade v.褪色、逐渐消失

prospect n.前景、设想

remote adj.遥远的

scant adj.不足的、缺乏的

take stock (of sth.) 对…加以总结

look in the mirror 反思

vow n.誓言 vow to

corollary n.必然的结果、推论

eg. Is social inequality the inevitable corollary of economic freedom?

社会不平等是不是经济自由的必然结果

bring sth. to heel 使听话

eschew v.（有意的）避开

periodically adv.定期的

instil v.逐渐灌输

compassion n.同情、怜悯

foster v.促进

ascetic adj.禁欲的、

dreary adj.沉闷的、枯燥无味的

be richly flavored 味道浓郁、有丰富调味的

stir v.搅动

simmer v.用文火炖、煨

aroma n.香味

tangy adj.有浓烈香味的

velvety adj.柔软光滑的

Velvety texture

velvet 天鹅绒

ponder v.思考、琢磨

appetite n.胃口、食欲

nudge v.轻推、鼓励

forswear 许诺放弃、发誓戒除

Sentence：

Short days,long nights, freezing temperatures,Christmas a swiftly fading memory and the prospect of spring remote.

January is the season of looking in the mirror and take stock,of regret,determination and abstinence.

Shiro,for instance,is a stew made from chickpea flour,stirred into hot water and seasoned with berbere–a characteristic Ethiopian mixture usually containing ground dried chillies,black peppercorns and spices such as cinnamon, ginger and cumin.

As it simmers,it fills the house with an earthy remix of Christmas aromas.
