IELTS写作素材：remote learning

This year proved once and for all: screens are no substitute for real life.

words:

maximalist n.最高纲领主义者 tech maximalist 科技总忠实的拥趸

sell sth. to sb. 说服某人接受（观点、意见）

physical classroom n.实体教室

summon vt.传唤、召唤

keystroke n.敲击键盘

sleek adj.造型优美的；时尚的

remote learning 远程学习

exacerbate vt.使…恶化、使…加剧

sequester vt.使隔绝、隔离

sentences:

The internet offered unlimited possibilty.Information was everywhere, easily summoned at a keystroke.

Students of the 21st-century classroom only need lessons uploaded to their sleek tablets where their teacher’s face will be trapped.

In western countries,it fueled a huge controversy that the pandemic shifted physical education towards online learning,because not all students have functioning internet adn some students live in a chaotic household unsuitable for study,which has widened the already yawning education gap.

Yawning inequality gaps have only been exacerbated,with+n+doing.

Professors,meanwhile, will be eager to resume life inside a classroom,where lively discussions and genuine learing can take place naturally without the mediator of a Zoom screen.

The pandemic made it easy to imagine an approaching dystopia: one in which,in the coming years, we would all sequester ourselves away from light and air, too terrified to venture outdoors. Instead of heading to the bar or the gym, we would build worlds of our own within our four walls, content to approximate the reality we once knew.

大流行使人们很容易想象一个即将到来的反乌托邦：在未来几年中，我们都将自己与光和空气隔离开来，害怕到户外冒险。我们不会去酒吧或健身房，而是在四堵墙内建立自己的世界，满足于接近我们曾经知道的现实。

Instead, we rediscovered parks and trails, flocked to beaches,and revolutionized city streets with outdoor dining. We wearied of our devices.There is no app or program that can replicate a friend’s laughter across the table or a teacher’s lesson at the front of a classroom.After the pandemic, in a post-vaccination world, we will race back to our old lives.Zoomworld will belong to history.

相反，我们重新发现了公园和小径，涌向海滩，并通过户外用餐彻底改变了城市街道。我们厌倦了我们的设备。没有任何应用程序或程序可以复制朋友在桌子上的笑声或老师在教室前面的课程。大流行之后，在疫苗接种后的世界里，我们将回到过去，Zoomworld将属于历史。
