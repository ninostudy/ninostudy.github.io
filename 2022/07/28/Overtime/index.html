Overtime

words:

spur vt.刺激、加速

chief n.(公司或机构)最高领导人

endorse vt.赞同、支持、背书

intensely competitive industry 竞争激烈的行业

accumulate vt.积聚 accumulate personal wealth.

hashtag n.标签

trending topic 热搜

rung n.等级、地位、阶梯

Netflix-like

such as = from … to

Tech giants such as Facebook and Google = Tech giants from Facebook to Google

have endorsed the practice as necessary for survival in an intensely competitive industry and the key to accumulate personal wealth.

After plunging on monday,the company’s share price rallied the following day,implying that the chairman’s speech has restored the confidence of investors.

Hashtags from laborer to hero in harm’s way - especially the frontline workers batting against the pandemic - have spurred debates on Twitter-like Sino Weibo service.
